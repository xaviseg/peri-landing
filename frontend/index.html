EMBRACE YOUR JOURNEY

Perimenopause is a natural transition — but it can feel confusing and unpredictable. This journal helps you understand what’s happening in your body and how small, evidence-based lifestyle choices can make a real difference. Research shows that healthy habits like movement, balanced nutrition, stress management, and sleep support can ease symptoms and improve long-term wellbeing. Additionally, being proactive about your health can reduce the risk of chronic disease. This journal is based on the pillars of lifestyle medicine and invites you to take a holistic, compassionate approach to your health — one day at a time.
